Tomato Soup

Description

This creamy tomato soup is not only easy to make but tastes amazing as well. This soup is so easy it only takes 5 minutes to prepare and 20 minutes to cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 quart tomato juice
  • salt to taste
  • 2 cups milk

Steps

  1. In a Dutch oven, over medium heat, saute onions in butter until translucent. Remove from the heat.
  2. Stir in the flour so that no lumps remain, then slowly whisk in the tomato juice. Return to the heat and add salt to taste.
  3. Cook until just boiling but turn off the heat before it boils.
  4. Let cool 10 minutes then slowly stir in milk.
  5. Serve immediately.
